📉 Variance
—
Actual − Forecast
Hours Variance (Actual − Forecast)Difference between what was actually charged vs. what was forecasted. Positive = over-burn (charged more than forecast). Negative = under-burn. Calculated as: Actual Hours to Date minus Forecasted Hours to Date.Formula: Actual Hrs − Forecasted Hrs · Source: FLEX ETC report
📈 Utilization %
—
Act / Forecast
Utilization RatePercentage of forecasted hours that have been actually consumed. 100% means perfectly on track. Above 100% means over-utilized (burning faster than forecast). Below 100% means under-utilized.Formula: (Actual Hrs ÷ Forecasted Hrs) × 100 · Source: FLEX ETC report
